Race: Half-Elf
Class: Rogue

Her mother, a talented bard, was murdered when Thalia was just a child, leaving her to fend for herself on the streets. She grew up in the shadows, learning the ways of the city and mastering the art of stealth. Her father's identity remains a mystery, fueling her determination to uncover the secrets of her past. Personality:

Thalia is cunning, resourceful, and fiercely independent. She has a sharp wit and a quick tongue, often using humor to deflect from her troubled past. Despite her tough exterior, she has a soft spot for children and the downtrodden, always willing to go out of her way to help them. Thalia is slow to trust, but once she does, she is a loyal friend and ally.

Appearance:

Thalia is a lithe and agile half-elf, standing at 5'6" with a slender build. She has striking emerald green eyes that seem to pierce through the darkness and shoulder-length raven-black hair often tied back in a loose braid. Her skin is fair, and she bears a few scars from her life on the streets. Thalia dresses in dark, practical clothing, favoring a hooded cloak that helps her blend into the shadows.




Description:

Thalia moves with the grace of a cat, her every step silent and deliberate. She has an air of confidence and mystery, her eyes constantly scanning her surroundings. Her voice is soft but firm, carrying an authority that demands attention. She often keeps to the shadows, observing before she acts, always calculating her next move.
