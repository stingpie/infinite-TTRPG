Malthusius is an evil sorcerer who has increased cloud cover and, as a side effect, decreased crop yields. His motives are as enigmatic as the riddles he dispenses like candy. He is competetive and smart, and can very easily stump the most wise of wizards. Those who fail to solve his riddles are met with a terrible fate; They are turned into newts and put into a terrarium! For this reason, no one has been willing to stand up to him, until now.
