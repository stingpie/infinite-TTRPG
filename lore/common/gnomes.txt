Gnomes are a small, ingenious _race_ known for their curiosity, creativity, and vibrant personalities. Standing between 3 and 4 feet tall, gnomes have slender builds, expressive features, and a twinkle of mischief in their eyes. Their skin tones range from earthy browns to fair pinks, and their hair comes in a variety of colors, often worn in playful styles.

Gnomes excel in invention, alchemy, and illusion _magic_. Their communities, typically found in forests, hills, or underground burrows, are bustling with intricate gadgets, whimsical contraptions, and alchemical experiments. Gnomes value innovation and delight in solving problems with clever, unconventional solutions.

Their society is organized into tight-knit family units and guilds, where knowledge and skills are shared freely. Gnomes are known for their friendliness and hospitality, always ready to share a story or a new invention with visitors.
